Salesforce Workshop

Workshop covering Salesforce ecosystem, UI navigation, data modeling, workflows, automation with Process Builder, and career opportunities

Date August 2, 2025
Time 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM
Location CE Department
Category Workshop

About the Workshop

The Salesforce Workshop was a comprehensive technical session designed to introduce students to the Salesforce ecosystem and its various components. Conducted by industry expert Mr. Sanjay Khajure, Sr. Salesforce Developer from MindzCloud Technologies, this workshop provided hands-on experience with one of the world's leading CRM platforms.

Participants gained practical knowledge of Salesforce's user interface, data modeling techniques, workflow automation, and career opportunities in the Salesforce ecosystem. The 5-hour intensive workshop balanced theoretical concepts with practical demonstrations, giving attendees a solid foundation in Salesforce development.

Workshop Topics

Salesforce Ecosystem

Comprehensive overview of the Salesforce platform and its various components.

Key Learning Areas:

  • Introduction to Salesforce CRM
  • Cloud offerings and platform capabilities
  • Salesforce architecture and security model
  • Navigation and user interface overview
  • AppExchange and third-party integrations
  • Career paths in Salesforce ecosystem
Industry Insights Platform Overview Career Guidance

Data Modeling & Automation

Hands-on session covering data management and workflow automation in Salesforce.

Key Learning Areas:

  • Object relationships and data modeling
  • Validation rules and data quality
  • Workflow rules and approval processes
  • Process Builder for automation
  • Introduction to Flow automation
  • Best practices in Salesforce development
