About Model Mystery

The ACM Student Chapter organized a 3D Modeling Event with a workshop on November 2nd, followed by a competition on November 4th. The event aimed to enhance students' skills in engineering drawings and 3D printing using Fusion 360.

It included a pre-round quiz and a final round where teams designed 3D models based on selected problem statements.
