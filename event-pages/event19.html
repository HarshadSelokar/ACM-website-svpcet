Power BI Workshop

A hands-on introduction to Power BI for creating interactive dashboards and professional data reports

Date January 30, 2025
Duration 2 Hours
Venue BF-05
Participants 70 Students

About the Workshop

The Power BI Workshop, organized by the Department of Computer Engineering in collaboration with the ACM Student Chapter on January 30, 2025, provided 70 second-year students from Section A with hands-on experience in data visualization and analysis. Led by Atharva Bhede (Technical Lead), the workshop focused on empowering participants to create interactive dashboards and professional reports using Power BI.

With guidance from Prof. Yogita Nikhare and student coordinators, the session included practical exercises on data extraction, transformation, and report generation. The interactive format and dedicated volunteer support ensured an engaging and effective learning experience, equipping students with valuable skills for data-driven decision-making.
