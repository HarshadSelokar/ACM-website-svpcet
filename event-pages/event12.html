N8N : ai agent workflow

Upcoming

Unlock the power of automation with n8n, the open-source workflow automation tool that puts you in control. In this hands-on workshop, you’ll learn how to create powerful automations that connect your favorite apps and services – no coding required (but fully supported if you want to go deeper!). Whether you're a developer, marketer, data analyst, or just someone looking to save time on repetitive tasks, this workshop will give you the tools and skills to automate processes efficiently. What You’ll Learn: Introduction to n8n and its core features Building your first workflows step-by-step Integrating with popular services (like Slack, Gmail, Notion, Airtable, APIs, and more) Creating conditional logic and using variables Best practices for debugging and scaling workflows Hosting your own n8n instance (for advanced users) Who Should Attend: Beginners to intermediate users who are interested in workflow automation, process optimization, and integrating different tools with ease. What to Bring: A laptop and curiosity! No prior experience with n8n is required. Takeaways: By the end of the workshop, you’ll walk away with fully functional automated workflows, hands-on experience with n8n, and the confidence to build your own custom automations.
