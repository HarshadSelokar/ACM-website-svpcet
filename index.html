Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to excellence in technology education

Our Vision

To be a leading ACM Student Chapter that fosters innovation, technical excellence, and professional growth among computer engineering students. We envision creating a vibrant community where students can explore technologies, develop leadership skills, and contribute meaningfully to the field of computing.

Our Mission

To provide students with comprehensive learning opportunities through workshops, seminars, and hands-on projects. We aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements while promoting collaborative learning, technical innovation, and professional networking within the computing community.

Who We Are

Empowering Future Tech Leaders

The ACM Student Chapter at SVPCET was established in 2019 to cultivate a vibrant community of technology enthusiasts. Our mission is to provide students with opportunities to enhance their skills, collaborate on innovative projects, and engage with industry leaders.

We organize seminars, workshops, and technical talks, bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world application. Through networking opportunities and hands-on experience, we empower students to become proficient professionals.
